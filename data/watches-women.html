G-SQUAD

G-SQUAD

The G-SQUAD series is a training partner and cool fashion accessory in one. These watches feature a sensor that measures your daily steps and syncs all of this data with your smartphone via Bluetooth®. You can then check the number of steps you've walked and calories you've burned using the G-SHOCK Connected app. It's a great way to help you keep track of your fitness level.

CLASSIC

UNIQUE AND ORIGINAL SINCE 1983

Different shapes, trendy patterns and stylish designs. With its corners and edges, this G-SHOCK model quickly became an international favourite among urban youth. All the designs in the CLASSIC collection have one thing in common: They are always strong shock-resistant and almost indestructible. Whether at work, playing sport or enjoying leisure activities, a CLASSIC watch handles even the most extreme conditions with ease. It offers the perfect combination of ultimate toughness, unique design and trendy street style.
